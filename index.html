About Me

Hi! Nice to have you here, in my little space on the internet!

I'm Rakshith Ashok. I got infatuated with Digital Art & Design back in 2006, and have been evolving as a Creative ever since!

I have a penchant for vibrant colors and imagery. Being quite detail oriented, I strive hard to make sure my work is pixel perfect.

By day, you'll find me working on design projects, experimenting on my art or reading up on the latest trends in contemporary Art, Design, Architecture and Fashion.

By night, you'll find me working on my music, cranking out beats & mixes.

I'm always up for interesting projects or creative collaborations. So, if you have any ideas, or would like to commission art work, drop me a line!

Music

Music is a very important part of my life. It's the best drug that's out there! Music is that powerful tool, which can express emotions, that one simply can't put into words. I enjoy producing it, as much as I enjoy listening to it. I'm a night owl, and when the sun goes down, I start cranking out mixes, beats and random melodies, mostly out of spontaneity. I'm highly passionate about it, and I'm stoked that I get to share my work with you!
